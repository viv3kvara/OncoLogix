About OncoLogix

The Tumor Registry is dedicated to improving outcomes for cancer patients by providing a global platform for pathology diagnosis. Our mission is to compile and analyze microscopic tumor images to help researchers, doctors, and students advance in medical sciences.

With a growing collection of expertly annotated tumor cases, we aim to create a collaborative community for knowledge sharing and innovative research.

Who We Are

The **World Tumor Registry™ (WTR™)** is a non-profit 501(c)(3) international organization based in Pittsburgh, USA. Founded in 2021, its mission is to create a **comprehensive, searchable, open-access catalog** of digital microscopic images of human cancer types and subtypes.

WTR is structured as a **collection of registries** for individual cancer sites (e.g., thyroid, lung, breast cancer). Each registry is governed by an **Editorial Board** consisting of 20 expert pathologists from various regions, ensuring global representation and knowledge sharing.

Our **Advisory Council**, made up of internationally recognized pathologists, guides research efforts. Additionally, a **Board of Directors** oversees WTR operations, ensuring inclusivity and diversity in cancer research.
